Allergy Information

We understand the importance of providing accurate information about allergens in our products. Please see below for information about common allergens:

  • Milk: Our milkshakes contain dairy and may not be suitable for individuals with lactose intolerance.
  • Nuts: Some of our milkshakes may contain nuts or traces of nuts. Please inquire with our staff if you have nut allergies.
  • Gluten: While we do not use gluten-containing ingredients in our milkshakes, cross-contamination may occur in our kitchen. Please exercise caution if you have gluten intolerance or celiac disease.
  • Soy: Some ingredients in our milkshakes may contain soy. Please check with our staff if you have soy allergies.
